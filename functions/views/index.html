Welcome
The Ocean Rose is a modern, unique family home with amazing ocean views situated just steps from Seacliff beach in Aptos. This beautifully furnished vacation home has vaulted ceilings, hardwood, slate and carpeted floors, lots of windows and skylights, two ocean view decks, indoor and outdoor dining, and beautiful landscaping. The home has beautiful ocean views from almost every room and you can hear the gentle crash of the waves while lying in bed.
